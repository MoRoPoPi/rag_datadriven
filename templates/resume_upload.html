Resume Matching

Upload your resume to find job opportunities that match your skills and experience. Our system will analyze your resume using advanced AI techniques and recommend relevant job postings.

Supported formats: PDF, TXT, DOCX

How it Works

  1. Upload your resume in PDF or text format
  2. Our system uses OCR technology to extract text from your resume
  3. The content is analyzed using a Retrieval Augmented Generation (RAG) system
  4. Matching job postings are ranked by relevance to your skills and experience
  5. Review your personalized job recommendations
